Stakeholders: Commercial mapping services & the web

PeterR
2020-10-03

At the joint W3C-OGC Maps for the Web workshop, we held a panel that brought together commercial Web mapping platforms to discuss issues important to their operations that relate to Web standards and browsers.

The panel included Thomas Lee from MapBox, Ed Parsons of Google, Johannes Lauer with HERE maps and Daniel Lewis of Geotab. The panel was moderated by Ted Guild of the W3C.
