Indoor maps & floorplans

AmeliaBR
2020-09-28

Claudia Loitsch & Julian Striegl are presenting a talk at the Maps for the Web workshop on Indoor Maps (maps / floor plans of buildings & other indoor spaces). They’ll focus on the requirements for accessibility by visually impaired people, which is their primary area of research at the Dresden University of Technology.

  • powerpoint slides for the presentation
  • Video to be linked when available

They’ll be talking about the Accessible Maps project.

There are of course other reasons (beyond accessibility) to want better web map support for indoor maps, so I’d be interested in hearing comments on the more general topic, too.
